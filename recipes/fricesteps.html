Home Fried Rice

Preparation

  • Prepare all the ingredients before you start cooking, the cooking process is very quick and you will not have time to prepare while you are cooking
  • If you are cooking a large amount of then cook the ingredients in batches setting aside until the rice is ready to put together.

Cooking

  1. Heat oil – Heat about 1 1/2 tbsp oil in a wok or 2 tbsp oil in a skillet over high heat until smoking.
  2. Whisk the eggs. Pour in egg mixture and cook it through properly as a flat omelet. Remove from wok and set aside.
  3. Cook the chicken and prawns quickly, do not over cook. Cook in batches set aside
  4. Cook vegetables (still frozen is fine). Cook for 2 minutes so they defrost and water evaporates. Do not over cook, remove and set aside as necessary
  5. Cook bacon until is light golden – about 1 1/2 minutes. Add garlic and onions cook quickly continually stiring.
  6. Begin adding the rice heating through. Season with fish sauce as required while you cook
  7. Add vegtables and preprepared meats, again season with soy sauce, oyser sauce and fish sauce to taste.
  8. Add green onion then stir the chopped egg omelet into the rice.
  9. Remove from heat, stir through sesame oil and serve immediately!
